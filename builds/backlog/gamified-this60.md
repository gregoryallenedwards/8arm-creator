## What It Is

A gamified version of the This 60 ADHD focus app (https://apps.apple.com/gb/app/this-60-adhd-focus-app/id6741018069). This 60 is a tile-based ADHD focus tool. The idea is to make it closer to Finch (the self-care pet app) — adding game mechanics as a game developer would, bringing in RPG-style progression, rewards, and engagement loops that support rather than exploit ADHD tendencies.

## Why Build This

- Mentioned repeatedly as top candidate for first build
- Clear target audience with genuine pain (r/ADHD — actively seeking externalized executive function)
- Maps to white-label game thesis (gamify any self-help tool)
- Good content story: experienced game developer applies LiveOps + economy design thinking to a productivity app
