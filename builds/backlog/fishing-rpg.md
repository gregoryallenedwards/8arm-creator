**Status:** backlog
**Category:** Game / Joy
**Priority:** medium

## What It Is

An incremental clicker game with a strong RPG component. The character fishes for you even while you're away from the app (idle mechanic). Inspired by: Fishing World of Fishers, Dredge (golf-like fishing mechanic), fish storage + boat upgrade loop.

Free. Start easy.

## Reference Apps

- Fishing: World of Fishers (incremental)
- Fish & Tides
- Dredge (golf-like casting mechanic + fish storage)
- FishAngler

## Key Mechanics

- Idle fishing — character fishes while you're away
- Fish storage management
- Boat capability upgrades
- RPG progression layer
